Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At FaceChat, we respect your privacy and your right to delete your personal data. This policy outlines how you can request the deletion of your account and associated data, and what happens during the deletion process.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be erased from our servers. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.

1. What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Account Profile: Username, authentication credentials, and account settings
  • Call History: Records of all video calls and call metadata
  • Chat Messages: All text messages sent during calls
  • Device Information: Associated device tokens and registration data
  • Preferences: Notification settings and user preferences
  • Backups: All backup copies of your data
⚠️ Note: Video and audio streams are not permanently stored on our servers, as they are transmitted in real-time. However, any logs referencing these calls will be deleted upon account deletion.

2. What Data May Be Retained

In certain circumstances, we may be required to retain limited information:

  • Legal Compliance: Data required by law or court order
  • Fraud Prevention: Information needed to prevent abuse or verify identity during the deletion process
  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized, non-identifiable statistical data used to improve our service (cannot be linked to you)
  • Backup Archives: Data in backup systems may take up to 90 days to be completely purged

3. How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the FaceChat application
  2. Go to Settings or your account menu
  3. Select Account or Profile
  4. Scroll down to find "Delete Account" or "Remove Account"
  5. Tap to initiate the deletion process
  6. You will be asked to confirm your action (usually by entering your password)
  7. Read the warning about permanent deletion and confirm
  8. Your account deletion will be processed immediately

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot delete your account through the app, you can request deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to: support@facechat.app
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your username or registered email address
    • Full name (if provided during registration)
    • Phone number associated with the account (if applicable)
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  4. We will verify your identity and process the deletion
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Method 3: In-App Support Request

  1. Open the FaceChat application
  2. Navigate to Help or Support
  3. Select "Request Data Deletion"
  4. Follow the guided form to submit your request
  5. You will receive a confirmation within 24 hours

4. Deletion Timeline

Processing Timeline:

  • Immediate: Account is deactivated and no longer accessible
  • 24 Hours: Personal data is deleted from active databases
  • 7 Days: Backup copies are scheduled for deletion
  • 90 Days: Final purge of all backup archives is complete

In most cases, deletion will be completed within 7 days. However, some data retention in backup systems may extend up to 90 days for data integrity and compliance purposes.

5. Verification Process

To protect your account from unauthorized deletion, we implement a verification process:

  • Password Confirmation: You will be asked to re-enter your password
  • Email Verification: A confirmation link will be sent to your registered email
  • Cooling-Off Period: You may have a brief grace period to cancel the deletion request
ℹ️ Note: These safeguards ensure that only the legitimate account holder can request deletion.

6. Post-Deletion

After Deletion, You Will:

  • Receive a confirmation email confirming successful deletion
  • No longer be able to log into your account
  • Be unable to access any previous calls, messages, or account data
  • Lose access to any paid features or credits (which cannot be refunded)
  • Be able to create a new account with the same email address (after 30 days, if applicable)

What Happens to Your Contacts:

  • Your account will be removed from other users' contact lists
  • Previous conversations will be archived on their devices but marked as "Contact Deleted"
  • They will not receive notification of your deletion

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Data Shared with Other Users

If you have shared personal information with other users (e.g., through direct messages), we can only delete our copies. Users who received your messages may retain them in their local message history.

7.2 GDPR "Right to Be Forgotten" (EU Users)

Under GDPR Article 17, EU residents have the right to erasure. Deletion requests will be processed in compliance with GDPR requirements.

7.3 CCPA "Right to Delete" (California Users)

California residents can request deletion of personal information as provided under CCPA. We will process such requests within 45 days.

7.4 Dispute or Investigation

If your account is under investigation for abuse or violations of our Terms of Service, deletion may be delayed until the investigation concludes. We will notify you of any holds on deletion.

8. Preventing Accidental Deletion

If you accidentally request deletion:

  • Immediately check your email for the confirmation link
  • Do not click the confirmation link
  • Contact support within 24 hours to cancel the request
  • Your account will be preserved without deletion
⚠️ Important: Once you confirm the deletion request via email link or in-app confirmation, the process cannot be stopped. Your data will be permanently deleted.

9. Data Deletion Confirmation

After your account is deleted, you will receive an email confirmation that includes:

  • Confirmation that your account has been deleted
  • The date and time of deletion
  • Information about the types of data that were deleted
  • Confirmation that backup deletion will be completed within 90 days

10. Contact & Support

If you have questions about data deletion or your request status:

Email: support@facechat.app
Subject: Data Deletion Inquiry
Response Time: We aim to respond within 24-48 hours

11. Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Honoring all legitimate deletion requests promptly
  • Ensuring complete removal of your personal data from our systems
  • Complying with all applicable data protection laws and regulations
  • Providing transparency about what data is retained and why
  • Protecting your privacy even after deletion

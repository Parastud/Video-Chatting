Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to FaceChat ("we", "us", "our", or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Username: You provide this when creating your account
  • Authentication Credentials: For secure login and session management
  • Contact Information: Email or phone number (if provided for account recovery)

1.2 Communication Data

  • Video/Audio Streams: Real-time media during video calls
  • Chat Messages: Text messages sent during video calls
  • Call Metadata: Call duration, participants, timestamps, and room information

1.3 Device Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address and connection information
  • Camera and microphone access status

1.4 Usage Data

  • Features accessed and how frequently
  • User interactions within the application
  • Error logs and crash reports

1.5 Permission-Based Data

When you grant permissions, we may access:

  • Camera: For video streaming during calls
  • Microphone: For audio streaming during calls
  • Contacts: To help you find and connect with other users (if applicable)
  • Storage: For caching and temporary files

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: Enable video calling, messaging, and account management
  • Authentication & Security: Verify your identity and protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Communication: Send important notifications about your account and service updates
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Safety: Detect and prevent abuse, spam, and malicious activity

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication using JWT tokens
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

Note: While we strive to use reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward
  • Call Logs & Chat History: Retained for 30 days unless you request deletion
  • Video/Audio Streams: Not permanently stored; transmitted in real-time and not recorded
  • Error Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the platform (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Protection of Rights: To prevent fraud, abuse, or other harm
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or business sale (with privacy protections)

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.

6.2 Correction

You can update or correct your account information at any time through the app settings.

6.3 Deletion

You can request complete deletion of your account and associated data. Refer to our Data Deletion Policy for more details.

6.4 Opt-Out

You can control notification preferences in your account settings.

7. Third-Party Links & Services

Our application may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies independently.

8. Children's Privacy

FaceChat is not intended for individuals under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete this information immediately and take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the application. Your continued use of FaceChat after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: support@facechat.app
Address: We are located and operate globally, with data centers in multiple regions.
Response Time: We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

11. Additional Disclosures

11.1 GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability, the right to be forgotten, and the right to object to processing. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

11.2 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to know what information is collected, delete personal information, and opt-out of the sale of personal data (which we do not engage in).
